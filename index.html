About Us

Building the
future, one
project at a time.

NOOCON is a Belgrade, Serbia-based Venture Studio using AI to launch website and web-based projects quickly. We rely on popular AI tools to quickly go from ideation to launch.

Our projects include content/news websites and newsletters, as well as SaaS applications. The company is led by Dusan Belic, who has been tinkering with the Internet since 1996.

During the years, he has launched a number of his own projects and led various ventures for clients across the world.
